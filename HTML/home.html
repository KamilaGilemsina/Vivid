We are Vivid Squad.

An anonymous collective of digital designers and visual artists based across the globe. We transform bold ideas into striking visual experiences—from branding and illustrations to immersive digital art and motion design.

For over three years, we've operated under a shared identity, blending creativity with anonymity to focus purely on the art. We collaborate with forward-thinking clients to build unforgettable visual identities and push the boundaries of digital design.
