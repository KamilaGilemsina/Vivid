NEON DREAMS

A living, breathing digital canvas where light, sound, and motion converge.

NEON DREAMS is an immersive, real-time interactive experience that transforms the browser into a dynamic art space. Built as a fusion of generative visuals and responsive audio, the project invites visitors to shape their own ethereal environments through movement, clicks, and sound.

Using the microphone and mouse as inputs, the installation responds to ambient noise, voice, and interaction-rendering pulsating waves, fluid particle systems, and neon-drenched landscapes that evolve with every engagement. The visuals are driven by custom GLSL shaders, creating hypnotic gradients, glitch effects, and organic light trails that feel almost alive.

The experience is designed to be both meditative and playful, a digital sanctuary where technology meets emotion. It serves as a testament to the creative potential of real-time web graphics and audio reactivity, exploring how anonymity in creation can lead to universally personal moments of connection.

Perfect for exhibitions, digital showcases, or as a standalone piece of net art, NEON DREAMS blurs the line between user and artist-every session is unique, every interaction tells a story.
